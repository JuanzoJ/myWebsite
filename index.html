    Here are my tips if you want to start playing guitar:
  1. Acquire an acoustic or electric guitar based on your preference and budget.
  2. Start with fundamental chords like C, G, D, E minor, and A minor. Practice transitioning between them.
  3. Develop a basic strumming pattern with downstrokes and upstrokes to build rhythm.
  4. Choose simple songs that use the chords you've learned. This helps make practice enjoyable.
  5. Set aside regular time for practice. Focus on chord changes, strumming, and playing songs to build your skills.
